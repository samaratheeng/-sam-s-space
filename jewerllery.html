*jewellery

by using real cicadas wings that i found on my trip to Kathmandu, encased in resin and combined with my grandmothers old pearls, it was my aim to modify the heirloom given to me to better translate into my style, giving these old pearls some new life.

other explorations of using resin to preserve insects. Earrings made from the butterfly called the Common Mormon with distinct red and white spots on black. Pendants made using pieces of mirrors and common yellow fruit moths.

explorations of welding and metallurgy using a soldering iron, soldering flux, copper wire and aluminium wire. Pieces made with the combination of these materials with mirrors.
